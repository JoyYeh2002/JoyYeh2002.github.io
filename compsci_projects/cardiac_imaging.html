Cardiac Imaging

May 2023
EN.510.433 Medical Image Analysis

Objectives:

  • Given a sequence of cardiac ultrasound images (2-channel and 4-channel views), use medical image analysis techniques to locate the left ventricle of the heart
  • Then, re-construct 3D volumes of the left ventricle according to the orthogonal image views, then estimate the stroke volumes
  • Evaluate the algorithm on test data of 10 patients

    • Skills Applied:

  • Computer vision implementation wtih Python: dynamic thresholding, image denoising, template matching, and interpolation
  • Algorithm design, development, and testing
  • Data pipeline organization

    • Comments:

    The project has a largely open-ended task that allows me to apply any image analysis technique from the course material. This project adopted traditional computer vision techniques, rather than ML-based models, allowing me to gain a much deeper understanding of their practical applications. However, I'm curious about the performances of ML-based approaches after manually setting a-priori constraints and pre-processing.
